Complaints

Complaints procedure.

Our commitment is to provide a high standard of legal service. If something goes wrong, please tell us and we will do our best to put it right.

How to complain

Please contact Laura O’Neill in the first instance, by email at loneill@evunalegal.com or by telephone on +44 (0)7386 440854. Setting out the nature of the complaint and the outcome you are seeking will help us respond promptly.

What happens next

We will acknowledge your complaint within five working days and will aim to provide a full written response within eight weeks. Where the complaint is more complex, we will keep you informed of progress.

If we cannot resolve your complaint

If we are unable to resolve your complaint to your satisfaction, or if eight weeks have passed since you first raised the matter, you may refer the complaint to the Legal Ombudsman.

You can contact the Legal Ombudsman by post at PO Box 6167, Slough, SL1 0EH, by telephone on 0300 555 0333, by email at enquiries@legalombudsman.org.uk, or via the website at legalombudsman.org.uk.

The Legal Ombudsman requires you to refer your complaint to them within six months of receiving our final response, and ordinarily within one year of the act or omission you are complaining about, or one year from when you should have realised there was cause for complaint.

Reporting a solicitor

If your complaint is about the professional conduct of a solicitor rather than the standard of service, you can report the matter to the Solicitors Regulation Authority. Their contact details are on the SRA website at sra.org.uk.
