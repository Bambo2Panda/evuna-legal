Privacy notice.

Last updated: 25 May 2026

This notice explains how Evuna Legal Consultancy Ltd (“we”, “us”) collects and uses personal data, in line with the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Evuna Legal Consultancy Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 17200544). Registered office: 227 Oving Road, Chichester PO19 7EJ. We act as the data controller for the personal data described below.

What we collect

  • Information you give us via the contact form or by email: name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Information you give us in the course of an engagement: contact details, instructions, and information necessary to provide legal services.

How we use it

  • To respond to enquiries and assess whether we can help.
  • To provide legal services under an engagement, and to comply with our regulatory and professional obligations.
  • To keep records as required by law and the Solicitors Regulation Authority.

Legal basis

We rely on legitimate interests (responding to enquiries), contract (providing services under an engagement), and legal obligation (regulatory record-keeping).

How long we keep it

Contact enquiries that do not lead to an engagement are deleted within 12 months. Engagement files are retained for the period required by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and our professional indemnity insurer, typically six years from the end of the matter.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share it with regulators, courts, or other professional advisers where required to provide services or comply with law. The website is hosted on GitHub Pages; basic server logs may be processed by GitHub Inc.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at loneill@evunalegal.com. You may also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office at ico.org.uk.

Cookies

This website does not set cookies and does not use analytics or tracking.

Changes

We may update this notice from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the date above.
